Retractableawnings is a residential and commercial awning company that designs, assembles and distributes diverse retractable awnings and canopies, commercial sun shades and free-standing awnings for commercial buildings. Being on the market for 33+ years we have learned how to keep standards high in terms of fabric and components quality, production, installation and customer service. So let us advise you on what to consider while choosing business awnings.
Think about functional and aesthetic purposes that commercial coverings serve to. What weather issues you face in your local area? What kind of weather conditions protection you need to have? What colors and design will appeal to your customers? Should coverings be attached to a building or stand free? The establishment of design and functions starts from here.
Be careful about products' structure and size, framing components and fabric If business awnings and canopies are made for sun protection and light precipitation, the water-resistant acrylic fabric is used here, but such a choice is completely inappropriate for "heavy" weather conditions. If your business building has somehow unique form, you'll probably need a custom design for commercial retractable awnings. This also can effect the process of installation and, in turn, warranty. If you buy commercial awnings online on our website and you need to know how certain covering will look on your building, feel free to request 3D rendering images. Research commercial awning prices
Understanding of commercial awning costs can be represented in one simple formula:
Low prices = low quality, including fabric, components and life expectancy. With no exception. In money equivalent, such a product will cost lower than $2000.

Mid-prices = mid/high quality, depending on the manufacturer.
High prices = high quality & awnings for buildings' longer life expectancy.
Prices also differ depending on product materials and functions.
Commercial awnings that serve to the sun and light precipitation protection cost from $2000 to $12000 varying on size, manufacturer and awning model.
Canopies for sun protection only would cost you less than $1000 and can reach $20000 for large coverage areas.
Waterproof covers for heavy precipitation like retractable louver roof systems prices start around $5000 and can increase up to $40000.
Exact prices depend on your sizes and models and can be estimated with help of an expert for every specific case. Also, you'd better use expert support in measuring areas where the covering will be situated to achieve accurate results.
